Group Project 1 : AllTrails AND Parks

This app was designed for any person who wanted to find a park close by to them by searching for any city in the world and getting information on parks and park names. It utilizes the MapBox API, LocationIQ API, as well as Bulma framework to assist with styling.

Team Members: Andrew Parsons, Brian Hernandez, Myles Magallanes.

Group Project 2 : Neighborhood Watch

This app allows a person to give a safety rating as well as a comment to a neighborhood in the San Diego area. Using a login feature, a person can see how safe a neighborhood is before they decide to travel there or even buy a home in that area.

Team Members: Arvin John, Nick Macaluso, William Lucht
